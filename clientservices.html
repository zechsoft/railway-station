Frequently Asked Questions
How long does the repair take?
Most screen replacements are completed in 45 minutes to 1 hour. However, timing may vary depending on the specific model and any additional damage that needs to be addressed.
Is there a warranty for this service?
Yes, all our screen replacements come with a 6-month warranty. This covers any manufacturing defects in the replacement parts but does not cover physical damage or water damage.
Do you offer doorstep repair services?
Yes, we offer doorstep repair for most services including screen replacements. There is an additional travel charge of ₹200-₹500 depending on your location.
